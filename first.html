The Asus ZenFone 5Z sports a 6.2-inch display with FullHD+ resolution and has a 19:9 aspect ratio. It is powered by the Snapdragon 845 processor that is clocked at 2.8GHz. It has 6GB of RAM and 64GB of internal storage which is further expandable upto 2TB. The phone runs on Android 8.0 Oreo with ZenUI 5 on top. It sports a dual camera setup at the back consisting of a 12-megapixel primary camera and an 8-megapixel secondary camera with a wide-angle lens. At the front, it has an 8-megapixel selfie camera. It has a 3300mAh battery and the company ships an 18W charger in the box. The ZenFone 5Z is a dual SIM device with a hybrid SIM slot and has support for Dual 4G and VoLTE.
